# Sherlock Methodology

Sherlock focuses on *behavior inference* from on-chain traces.

## What Sherlock Does

- Applies ownership and change heuristics.
- Builds and scores address clusters.
- Adds wallet fingerprint signals.
- Produces confidence-aware interpretations.

## Philosophy

Sherlock is the "investigator" of txray: transparent hypotheses, not black-box certainty.

## Output Style

Results include uncertainty and rationale, so analysts can audit every step.
